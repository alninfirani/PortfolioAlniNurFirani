I am a Learning & Development (L&D) and Instructional Design professional with over 5 years of experience building high-impact learning programmes — from Indonesia to Australia, across EdTech, corporate bootcamps, and international institutions.

I have personally led the end-to-end development of 200+ learning programmes — covering needs analysis, curriculum design, content production, QA, and post-launch improvement — ensuring every programme is grounded in pedagogy and delivers measurable learner outcomes.

I translate learning science (ADDIE, Bloom's Taxonomy, LXD) into scalable training systems that work — whether for self-paced e-learning, live bootcamps, or blended formats.
