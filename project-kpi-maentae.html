QA Principles

How I Approach Quality Assurance

🎯
1 Idea Per Slide
Every slide carries exactly one clear idea. Overlapping contexts flagged and restructured to support AREL format.
🔗
VO–Slide Alignment
Voice-over explains context shown on screen — reviewed for repetition, missing citations, and word-count compliance.
📐
Scaffolded Activities
H5P, worksheets, and exercises scaffold progressively — no duplication between activity types.
✅
Iterative Resolution
Feedback tracked per element — marked Done when resolved, suggestions provided for complex issues.
💬
Specific Feedback
Never vague — I reference exact sections, cite the standard violated, and give a clear suggestion.
🌐
Cross-cultural Review
Content reviewed for cultural appropriateness for Middle East market in F2F and Live Online formats.
